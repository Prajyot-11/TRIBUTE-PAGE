Here's a time line of Mahatma Gandhi

  • 1869 - Born in Gujarat, India.
  • 1883 - Married with Kasturba at Porbunder.
  • 1888 - Gandhi travels to London to train as Barrister.
  • 1891 - Gandhi returns to India.
  • 1894 - Founded the Indian National Congress.
  • 1906 - Gandhi introduces Satyagraha.
  • 1909 - Wrote 'Hind Swaraj' in Gujarati on board S.S.Kildonan Castle on the way to South Africa from London.
  • 1922 - Arrested. On 21 March sent to Yervada Jail. Remained in jail till March 1924.
  • 1930 - with 78 Ashramaties Gandhiji started his famous Dandi March to break the Salt Law.
  • 1932 - Gandhi concludes Salt March.
  • 1934 - Retires from politics.
  • 1936 - Founded Sevagram Ashram at Wardha
  • 1948 - Starts fasting for a cause.
  • 1948 - A bomb exploded in the midst of his prayer meeting at Birla House, Delhi.
  • 1948 - Gandhi is assasinated by Nathuram Godse.
